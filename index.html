How it works

This is web app in association with Tourism New Zealand to help tourists plan out how much it's going to cost them to rent out a car and drive around New Zealand.

All you need to do is input a little bit of information. First off, where you will be starting off on your journey and the end destination of your journey. Secondly, how many days you plan on travelling for and how many people are going on the trip. Lastly, put in what sort of car you plan on renting out.

Once all the information fields have been completed you will then see a map that shows your journey from A to B and directly below it will tell you how much it's going to cost for vehicle hire over hovever many days you chose, petrol costs for the distance you plan to travel and a summary of the total cost.
